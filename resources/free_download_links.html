2.  Free Download Manager

i
Technically, download managing and download accelerating are different things. However, software applications that go by the name/title "Download Manager" also provide download accelerating, and software applications that go by the name/title "Download Accelerator" also provide download managing. Therefore, although download managing and download accelerating are different things, with reference to software application names/titles, the terms "Download Manager" and "Download Accelerator" are synonymous.
!
Download managers are notorious for harboring adware, typically in the form of a banner near the top of the program's main user interface which is disabled once the user purchases/registers the program. The following download managers are some of the most popular. No claim is made as to whether they are freeware, trialware, adware, etc.. It is up to the user to do their own research before using them.
  • Download Accelerator Plus (speedbit.com)
  • FlashGet (flashget.com)
  • Free Download Manager (freedownloadmanager.org)
  • GetRight (getright.com)
  • Go!Zilla (gozilla.com) (Formerly Aureate Media/Radiate.)
  • Internet Download Manager (internetdownloadmanager.com)
